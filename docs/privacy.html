Privacy Policy

Wanderlist is an offline-first app. All data you enter (entries, notes, and preferences) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage and is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

We do not collect analytics, do not use tracking identifiers, and do not operate any backend server that receives your data. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it. There is no account, no sign-in, and no cloud sync in this app.
